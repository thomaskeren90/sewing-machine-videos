## 📋 COMPLETE WORKFLOW (Step by Step)

### STEP 1: GET PRODUCT IMAGES (15 minutes)

Go to these sites and save product images:

```
For each product in PRODUCTS.md:
1. Go to the Shopee/Tokopedia link provided
2. Save 2-3 clear product images per item
3. Save to folder: images/[product-code]/ 
   Example: images/A1-brother-gs2700/
```

**Folder structure:**
```
images/
├── A1-brother-gs2700/
│   ├── front.jpg
│   ├── side.jpg
│   └── accessories.jpg
├── A2-singer-4423/
│   └── ...
└── spare-parts/
    ├── C1-jarum/
    ├── C2-benang/
    └── ...
```

### STEP 2: GENERATE INDONESIAN VOICE (1 hour for 90 scripts)

**Using ElevenLabs (BEST QUALITY):**
1. Go to https://elevenlabs.io
2. Sign up (free account = 10,000 characters/month)
3. Go to "Speech Synthesis"
4. Select voice: Choose an Indonesian female voice
   - Search "Indonesian" in voice library
   - Pick one that sounds young and natural
5. Paste each script → Generate → Download MP3
6. Repeat for all 90 scripts

**Using CapCut (FREE & FAST):**
1. Open CapCut (app or desktop)
2. Create new project
3. Add text → paste script
4. Use "Text to Speech" → select Indonesian female voice
5. Export audio

**Pro tip:** For 90 videos, ElevenLabs Starter plan ($5/mo) gives you enough characters for all scripts.

### STEP 3: CREATE AI AVATAR VIDEOS (3-4 hours for 90 videos)

**OPTION A: HeyGen (BEST for talking avatar)**
1. Go to https://heygen.com
2. Sign up → Creator plan ($29/mo recommended for 90 videos)
3. Click "Create Video"
4. Choose avatar: Filter by "Female" + look for Asian/Indonesian-looking
5. Upload your voice MP3 from Step 2
6. OR paste script directly → HeyGen generates voice + avatar
7. Add product images as background
8. Add text overlay with price + contact
9. Export (vertical 9:16 for TikTok/Shorts)
10. Repeat

**Capacity:** Creator plan = 15 videos/mo. For 90 videos, you need Team plan ($69/mo) or buy credits.

**OPTION B: D-ID (CHEAPEST for avatar)**
1. Go to https://d-id.com
2. Sign up → Lite plan ($5.90/mo)
3. Upload a photo of Indonesian female (from free stock photo)
4. Upload voice MP3
5. AI makes the photo "talk"
6. Add background + text overlay
7. Export video

**Capacity:** Lite plan = 10 min video/mo. Enough for ~20-30 short videos.

**OPTION C: CapCut (100% FREE — No avatar, but text + images)**
1. Open CapCut
2. New project → 9:16 vertical
3. Add product images as slideshow
4. Add text-to-speech (Indonesian)
5. Add text overlays (price, CTA, contact)
6. Add music
7. Export

**This is FREE and UNLIMITED — just no talking avatar.**
