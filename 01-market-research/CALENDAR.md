## CONTENT REPURPOSING STRATEGY

For each of the 15 "base" videos:
1. **Full version** → YouTube (2-5 minutes)
2. **Short/clip version** → TikTok (15-60 seconds) 
3. **Behind-the-scenes** → Instagram Reels / TikTok bonus
4. **Static post** → Instagram carousel with key points

This way, 90 scripts can generate 180+ pieces of content.
